My films

Ashwatthama

Ashwatthama is a visualization of mythology manifested through the lens of modernity. It is a story of chaos and enigma that binds two discrete worlds together and in the process, puts forward a revelation that is influenced by a shared experience of reluctant collaboration where destiny has its say.
It got selected for around 13 film festivals in three years

Kauri (crow)

It was a story said in the third person perspective, where the main story was said by someone else inside the film. It was an experiment where we told a story on taking decisions and being assertive for it.
It was also made in 50 hours for world's largest filmmaking challenge, 50 hour filmmaking challenge by India Film Project, where our theme was WE ARE OUR DECISIONS.

Thank you

It's a heartfelt tale from the time of lockdown, where two queer people met online and share their emotions with each other, and their reality collides with certain consequences where love takes its own place and creates one new road for certain developments It was made in 50 hours for world's largest filmmaking challenge, 50 Hour filmmaking challenge by India Film Project, where our theme was LANGUAGE OF JOY and we were shortlisted in top 50 among 13000 entries.
